Privacy Policy for Tile Explorers

Last Updated: June 06, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Local Game Data

Tile Explorers does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

The game stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • Current level progress
  • High scores
  • Game completion status
  • Tutorial and onboarding preferences
  • Other gameplay settings

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Tile Explorers uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Cookies and similar technologies

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

4. Data Security

Game progress data is stored locally on your device. While we take reasonable measures, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Ad networks integrated with our app may use cookies, advertising identifiers, or similar technologies to serve relevant ads and measure advertising performance.

You can reset your device's Advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Google Settings > Ads).

Tile Explorers is family-friendly and designed for players of all ages.

We value your trust. Since we do not collect personal information online, your game progress data remains stored locally on your device.

However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
